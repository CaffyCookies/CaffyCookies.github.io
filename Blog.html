    "August 10, 2023 What makes video games so enticing and entertaining?

    Video games have many graphics and different sorts of stories that can entertain many different interest groups. With the many possibilities it allows people to consistently be surprised or experience new things which can give a sense of achievement. That sense of achievement can be accomplished as well by the game creating goals or milestones that often give a dopamine boost from completion.

    https://www.nytimes.com/2020/06/11/style/why-people-love-games.html

    According to the article video games have such interest due to the choices and actions we control in an environment that only simulates consequence rather than having actual risk. As well as having a sense of importance made from the fake consequence, video games abuse the system of competition which as seen through all of history is an extremely powerful driving force and motivator. The utility of competition is almost endless, as others develop their skills they will be matched with a challenge and the consistent challenge allows games to hold people's interest for extended periods of time.
